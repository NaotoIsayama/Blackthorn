Where stories live on skin

At Blackthorn Ink, every tattoo tells a story. Founded by award-winning artist Evelyn Blackthorn, our Edmonton studio blends the precision of fine art with the bold tradition of classic tattooing.

We believe a tattoo should be more than ink on skin — it should be a reflection of who you are, created with care, skill, and a touch of soul. That’s why every design we create is drawn from scratch, shaped through conversation, and refined to perfection before it ever meets a needle.

Evelyn Blackthorn is the founder and lead artist at Blackthorn Tattoo. Known for her bold linework and intricate floral designs, Evelyn has spent over a decade refining her craft and building a reputation for tattoos that balance strength and elegance. Her work blends traditional tattooing techniques with modern artistry, resulting in pieces that age beautifully and carry lasting meaning.

Beyond the chair, Evelyn is committed to creating a welcoming, professional studio environment where clients feel comfortable expressing their stories through ink.
