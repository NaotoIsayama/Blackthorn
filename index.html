My Story

I studied Fine Arts in University, but it wasn’t long before I realized tattooing was where I truly belonged. I apprenticed under a skilled artist in the Maritimes, Zack Harris, learning the craft from the ground up and developing a love for realism and black and grey work. Those years shaped not just my technique, but also my respect for the artistry and discipline behind every tattoo.

Now based in Edmonton, I run my own practice where I focus on creating detailed, lifelike tattoos that carry personal meaning for my clients.
