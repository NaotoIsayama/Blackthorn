Aftercare

After your session with me, your new tattoo will be covered with a Saniderm bandage. Leave this bandage on for the next 5 days. During this time, please avoid submerging the bandage in water, or prolonged contact with water while showering. After showering, simply pat the area lightly with a towel to dry.

During these 5 days, keep an eye out for any fluid buildup, redness, swelling, or any severe pain. While a small amount of dark fluid might buildup under the wrap, this is usually not a concern if it is a small amount. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you notice any of these signs.

After 5 days, you can begin to remove the bandage. First clean your hands and the area around the wrap with liquid antibacterial soap. Soak in warm water for a few minutes, and slowly peel off the bandage, starting from the top. Allow to area to air dry.

Over the next 3-4 weeks, gently apply some unscented lotion to the area twice a day. As well, avoid hot baths, pools hot tubs, saunas, and steam rooms while your new tattoo fully heals.
