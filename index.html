My Story

I’m Evelyn Blackthorn, and my journey into tattooing began in a fine arts studio, sketching and painting for hours every day. The precision, creativity, and storytelling I loved in traditional art naturally found a new canvas: skin. Over the years, I’ve honed my craft to create tattoos that are not just designs, but personal stories — art you carry with you forever.
